Marks, Wear

During the reunion, you and your friend kin will meet in a series of hugs. While all hugs are enjoyable, they also leave marks, some of them hurting, and some of them protective.

Spiny ♦️, Pokey ♠ - These types of creatures are more intense, leaving big marks when they hug. Unless protected, a hug from one of these should be memorable, one you will always rememeber when looking at your kin.

Softy ♥, Lumpy ♣ - These creatures hugs always bring some sort of protection. They create small marks, that can contain any future pain to a smaller location in your kin.
