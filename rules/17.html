Situating Play

Your niece has just turned 6 and she’s getting into the classics. Crisp booster pack in hand, you head over for a visit.

The two of you open it together excitedly, each pulling at its 4 corners. The cards spill out, her eyes widenening. The monsters are pretty cute. And one is foil!

Traveling

You’re out on your own on for the day, far away home. You meet a stranger and get to chatting, an hour or two evaporates.

They realize what time it is and pull a small card protector out of their pocket that you recognize immediately. You each lay your cards on the table. The winds aren’t in your favor today, and a few black sharpie marks later your new friend departs with your Lumpy 8.
