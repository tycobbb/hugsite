The Performance

This is not the only possible Performance of The Reunion, but it’s one you may want to use for your first game (or, perhaps all of them).

Place your Partner Kin face up in front of you. See them. See your friend’s Partner Kin. See your friend. If the two of you agreed on a greeting, exchange it now.

Shuffle your remaining four Kin and set them to the side face down. Choose the first player.

On the first player’s left, place three cards in a column. The first player places two cards on their side who give their friend’s kin a hug. The hugging kin, and player, mark the hugged kin according to their types.

On the first player’s right, place three cards in a column. The second player places two cards, and the first player’s kin receives a hug. The hugging kin, and player, mark the hugged kin.
